# Content Structure — The Talent+ Brief

## Overview
The Talent+ Brief is a monthly newsletter produced by the HR PID Team.
It is generated using a form tool that outputs formatted HTML for pasting into Gmail.
Each issue has two main sections plus a header and footer.
The form collects all content fields and renders a live preview.

## Header fields
- Issue number: a simple incrementing number e.g. Issue 1, Issue 2
- Month and year: e.g. April 2026
- Both are manually entered each month by the person filling in the form

## Intro block
- A short welcome or framing paragraph at the top of each issue
- Editable free text, 2-4 sentences
- For issue 1 this introduces the newsletter and invites people to share it
- From issue 2 onwards this will be a brief "what's in this issue" summary

## Section 1 — Talent+ Updates
This section covers changes made to the Talent+ system that month.
For issue 1 this is a retrospective covering January, February and March.
From issue 2 onwards it covers only that month's changes.

### How items are added
- The person filling in the form adds items one at a time using an "Add item" button
- Each item has three fields:
  1. Category: a dropdown with three options
     - System change
     - Process change
     - Behavior change
  2. Title: a short headline for the change, e.g. "Reference checks now in Talent+"
  3. Description: one sentence explaining what changed and why it matters to the reader

### Display rules
- Items are displayed in the order they are added
- Each item shows a colored dot indicating its category, followed by the bold title,
  followed by the description in regular weight
- A legend at the top of the section shows what each dot color means
- There is no grouping by month in issue 2 onwards — items are just listed
- For issue 1 only, items can optionally be grouped under month dividers
  (January / February / March) — this is a manual choice by the person filling in the form

### Removing items
- Each item has a remove button so mistakes can be corrected before copying

## Section 2 — People - Talent Workspace
This section covers the new platform being built by the HR PID team.

### Fields
- A single free text area for the monthly update paragraph
- 3-5 sentences describing what is happening with the project that month
- For issue 1 this introduces the project for the first time:
  what it is, why it matters, that it has just kicked off in April 2026
- From issue 2 onwards this will be a short progress update

### Display rules
- Shown as a card with a badge indicating project status
- Badge text for issue 1: "Kicked off April 2026"
- Badge text for future issues: "In progress" or similar — editable field

## Footer
- Fixed content, not editable each month
- Text: "Sent by the HR PID team · People and Culture Group, UNOPS"
- Second line: "Questions or feedback? Reply to this email."
- Third line: "Know someone who should receive this?
  Forward it and ask them to reach out to be added to the list."

## Output
- A single "Copy email HTML" button at the top of the tool
- Clicking it copies the complete formatted HTML to the clipboard
- The person then opens Gmail, creates a new email, and pastes
  using Ctrl+Shift+V to preserve formatting
- No other export or save functionality is needed
